Chorizo Tacos Recipe

Description

These are traditional street tacos, made with the fresh chorizo on corn tortillas and topped with some minimal toppings like onion, cilantro, radishes and lime juice. The only bad thing about them is it hard to stop at one or two or three. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 chorizo sausage
  • 2 tbsp chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 4 corn tortillas
  • 2 tbsp chopped onion
  • 2 tbsp chopped cilantro
  • 1 lime

Steps

  1. Mix chorizo and chipotle peppers in adobo sauce together in a bowl.

  2. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat; add chorizo mixture and cook until crisp, 5 to 7 minutes. Transfer chorizo to a plate, reserving grease in the skillet.

  3. Heat tortillas in the reserved grease in skillet over medium heat until warmed, 1 to 2 minutes per side. Stack 2 tortillas on top of each other for each taco. Fill with chorizo and top with onion, cilantro and lime.
